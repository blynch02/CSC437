Dynasty Overview

The San Francisco 49ers dominated the NFL throughout the 1980s, winning four Super Bowls between 1981 and 1989. Led by innovative head coach Bill Walsh and quarterback Joe Montana, the team revolutionized offensive football with the "West Coast Offense".
